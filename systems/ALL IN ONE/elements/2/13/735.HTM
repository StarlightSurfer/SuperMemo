    Lesson 65

    Not a baby

    不是一个孩子

    Listen to the tape then answer this question. Does Jill take the key to the front door?

    听录音，然后回答问题。吉尔有没有拿到大门的钥匙？

    FATHER: What are you going to do

    this evening. Jill?

    JILL: I'm going to meet some friends.

    Dad.

    FATHER: You mustn't come home late.

    You must be home at half past ten.

    JILL: I can't get home so early, Dad!

    JILL: Can I have the key to the front door please?

    FATHER: No, you can't.

    MOTHER: Jill's eighteen years old, Tom.

    She's not a baby.

    Give her the key.

    She always comes home early.

    FATHER: Oh, all right!

    FATHER: Here you are.

    But you mustn't come home after a quarter past eleven.

    Do you hear?

    JILL: Yes. Dad.

    JILL: Thanks, Mum.

    MOTHER: That's all right.

    Goodbye.

    Enjoy yourself!

    JILL: We always enjoy ourselves, Mum.

    Bye-bye.
